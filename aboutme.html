A little about myself

I am Nguyen Huu Truong Duy, a Japanese chef with 4 years of experience and a background in coding.

I studied Computer Science at Ho Chi Minh University of Science. In my third year, I moved to Japan to pursue greater opportunities. While in Japan, I continued to study software development independently, focusing on back-end and web development, alongside my training as a Japanese chef.

I consider myself a quick learner with a strong eagerness to explore new things. My proficiency in both English and Japanese allows me to access a broader range of resources and enhances my ability to work effectively in diverse environments.
